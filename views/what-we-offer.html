Personal Injury

  • Motor-Vehicle Accidents
  • Slip & Fall Injuries
  • Uninsured Motorist Claims
  • Motorcycle Accidents
  • Wrongful Death

Getting injured is unfortunate enough without having to suffer twice by being short-changed by the insurance company. Since the insurance company wants to pay you as little as possible, they have every incentive to treat you unfairly because their loyalty is to the insurance company they work for and their bottom line is profits not you. I will make sure you have aggressive and competent representation to insure you receive the maximum compensation that you deserve. You will be compensated for your past and future pain and suffering, mental suffering, lost wages, medical expenses and permanent impairment. There are issues surrounding subrogation with your health insurance, Medicare, Medicaid, Tricare and Medical Payments Coverage. They may be entitled to subrogation or they may not be depending on numerous factors. We may be able to find other insurance available for you that you have no idea you are entitled to based on your injuries. We may be able to stack policies and/or medical payment coverages. Finding competent doctors, dealing with healthcare providers or health insurance liens can be a confusing experience. You will be treated fairly and with dignity throughout this very difficult time in your life. Please do not hesitate to contact me any time. I am happy to answer your questions and/or schedule an initial consultation to discuss how I can be of service to you.
